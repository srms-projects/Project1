Homo Erectus

Neanderthals Conclusion

 Homo Erectus means "Upright Man" since they were the first hominid to be able to stand up-right.

 They were nomadic, meaning they didn't stay in one place for too long. They migrated out of Africa and went to Asia and possibly Europe.

 Homo Erectus lived for a very long time, having a livespan of about 1690000 years. They lived from 1.89 million BCE (BC) to about 200,000 BCE (BC)


Tools

 Homo Erectus were the first hominid to use advanced tools, they mostly used rocks to create other types of tools. They created a spear, hand-axe, hammer, and chopper.

Physical Features

 Homo Erectus, as stated before, was the first hominid to be able to stand upright. Their height ranged from about 4'9 to 6'1. Homo erectus had a brain size of about 900 cubic centimeters, 400 cubic centimeters less than our brains. Homo erectus had no chin and a round face.

Extra Information

 Homo erectus created carvings on shells

 They learned to control naturally occuring fire

 They added animal meat to their diets

 They were the first hominid to live outside of africa
