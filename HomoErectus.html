Homo Erectus

Neanderthals

 Homo Erectus means "Upright Man" since they were the first hominid to be able to stand up-right.

 They were nomadic, meaning they didn't stay in one place for too long. They migrated out of Africa and went to Asia and possibly Europe.

 Homo Erectus lived for a very long time, having a livespan of about 1690000 years. They lived from 1.89 million BCE (BC) to about 200,000 BCE (BC)
