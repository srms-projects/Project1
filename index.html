Neanderthals

Homo Erectus Conclusion

 Have you ever thought about the civilizations that came before us?

 The meaning of Neanderthal is "Man from Neander valley"

 They were nomadic, meaning they didn't live in one location for that long. They migrated from Asia to Europe.

 Neanderthals lived from 400,000 BCE to 40,000, living for 360,000 years.

Tools

 Neanderthals used spears and rocks as tools. They made clothing, spears, and hand-axes

Physical Features

 Males had an average of 5'5 and females had an average of 5'1. Their brain size was the same if not bigger than homo sapiens with an average of 1500 cubic centimeters. Neanderthals had a sturdier body structer than humans do today. Neanderthals had bigger noses than humans today have.

Extra Information

 They had a flute-like instrument

 They hunted for food

 They were the first hominids to bury their dead.

 They were the first hominid to create cothing

 They were the first hominid to be able to produce fire.

 They made cave art, ivory rings, animal teeth, and antlers.
